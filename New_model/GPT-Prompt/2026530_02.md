Family A — Geometry target and normalization redesign

Family B — Multi-modal probabilistic geometry head
Goal: replace deterministic regression with a distributional geometry head.
Try at least two of:

* mixture density network for free parameters and lattice
* K-component Gaussian / logistic mixture head
* quantile regression head
* discrete bins + residual regression
* latent class head followed by conditional regression
* ensemble of independently trained geometry heads

Family C — Geometry latent codebook / retrieval-prior model
Goal: learn reusable geometry modes instead of predicting all continuous values from scratch.
Try:

Family D — Complex-subset curriculum and reweighting
Goal: directly attack n_sites>=6 and n_sites>=12 collapse.

Family E — Stronger conditioning architecture
Goal: make geometry depend more explicitly on W/A and chemistry.
