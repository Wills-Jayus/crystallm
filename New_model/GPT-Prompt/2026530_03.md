Phase 3 — W/A decoder improvement campaign

After geometry is integrated, the main likely bottleneck is W/A generation. Run a long multi-strategy campaign. Do not stop after one failed attempt.

Minimum quota:
Run or implement at least 10 meaningful full-generation experiments across at least 4 strategy families. Tiny learning-rate changes do not count unless tied to a clear hypothesis.

Strategy family A — constrained W/A decoding
Try:

* legal OrbitToken mask under GT_SG
* formula-remainder-aware element decoding
* exact-cover feasibility during beam expansion
* canonical row order constraint
* hard row_count compatibility with formula and multiplicities
* beam search over row_count + W + A
* invalid partial candidate pruning

Goal:
Increase WA_hit@5 and formula_closure without damaging candidate diversity.

Strategy family B — row_count and skeleton-first decoding
Try:

* stronger row_count head
* separate skeleton/W decoder before element assignment
* top-N row_count candidates instead of single row_count
* skeleton beam then element beam
* multiset-stable WA key evaluation
* candidate diversity penalty for duplicate WA keys

Goal:
Avoid early row_count/skeleton collapse.

Strategy family C — train-prior / retrieval-prior W/A proposal
Try:

* train-set prior over legal row patterns conditioned on formula, SG, element set, atom count, crystal system
* row-conditioned W/A proposal from nearest train symbolic keys
* neural + retrieval hybrid candidate pool
* generated W/A candidates plus train-prior W/A candidates
* deduplicate by stable multiset key

Important:
This is allowed only as source-level W/A candidate generation. Do not use validation target WA. Do not use StructureMatcher labels for training.

Goal:
Improve WA_hit@5 and give e08 geometry better symbolic inputs.

Strategy family D — candidate selection without oracle labels
Try ranking generated candidates by:

* model log probability
* formula closure
* exact-cover validity
* row_count plausibility
* SG legality
* geometry likelihood from e07/e08
* retrieval distance
* no severe atom overlap
* lattice/volume plausibility
* minimum-distance sanity
* density or volume-per-atom sanity
* optional learned confidence head trained without StructureMatcher labels

Goal:
Improve match@1 and match@5 without oracle match labels.

Strategy family E — geometry-aware W/A decoding
Try:

* use geometry retrieval distance to guide W/A candidate ranking
* prefer W/A keys that have strong row-conditioned geometry support
* penalize W/A candidates whose retrieved geometry modes are inconsistent or low confidence
* generate W/A and geometry jointly in candidate space

Goal:
Avoid W/A candidates that are legal but geometrically unsupported.

Strategy family F — residual geometry after retrieval
If time permits, implement a neural residual head on top of e07/e08:

* input: formula, SG, W/A rows, retrieved geometry mode
* output: wrapped free-param residual and normalized lattice residual
* train only on clean_train
* evaluate on clean_val
* compare KNN-only vs KNN+residual

Goal:
See whether row-conditioned KNN can be improved further without post-hoc repair.
